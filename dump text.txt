VERSION 1.1.3

Gameplay Changes:
• Added [?] new state edicts.
• States with an unaccepted culture now cost more to maintain. 
• No longer able to convert culture in territories.
• Corruption increase from debasing currency increased from 2 to 2.5.
• Army Drill Gain Advisor bonus increased from 10% to -20%.
• Culture Conversion Cost Advisor bonus decreased from -25% to -15%.
• Logistics ideas: Garrison size bonus decreased from 75% to 33%.
• Reducing war exhaustion now decreases it by 2.5 up from 2.
• Advisor cost increase per year slightly boosted from 0.005 to 0.006.
• Advisor promotion cost increased from 60 months salary to 70.
• Merchant republic size limit increased from 20 to 22.
• Cost to remove accepted culture increased from 10 to 25.
• Liberty desire from historical rival modifier decreased from 50 to 40.
• Liberty desire from having over 300 development increased from 50 to 55.
• Liberty desire lost from losing an independance war increased from -50 to -65.
• Max colonies before forming a colonial nation increased from 4 to 6.
• Default autonomy after diplo-annex decreased from 60 to 30.
• Default autonomy after conquest increased from 50 to 75.
• Default autonomy after conquest of a core increased from 0 to 10.
Economic Changes:
• Devastation level in provinces after bankruptcy declaration increased from 10 to 15.
• Maximum price a province can be sold for increased from 100 to 200.
• Merchant speed decreased from 20 to 15.
• Base embargo efficiency decreased from 0.50 to 0.33
• Maximum mercantilism embargo efficiency boost decreased from 50% to 33%.
• Maximum build progress percentage to recover full cost when cancelling increased from 10% to 15%. 
• Prestige cost to downgrade a centre of trade increased from -10 to -20.

Military Changes:
• Combat dice limited to a maximum roll of 6.
• Base siege attrition further reduced from 0.95 to 0.80.
• Deaths upon a disease during a siege increased from 5% to 8%.
• Base defender loss rate during a siege assault increased from 5% to 12%.
• Base cavalry speed increased from 7 to 8.
• Maximum ducats able to be looted per month increased from 5 to 6.
• Warscore bonus upon completion of the wargoal increased from 5 to 8.
• Province occupation status flipped by fort time increased from 30 to 60.
